Our store

A jeweller you can actually talk to.

Modano is an independent jewellery shop on Sherbrooke West, in the middle of NDG. George, our owner, has spent decades in the trade — and still does the work himself, at the bench behind the counter.

That means you get a straight answer about what a piece needs, what it will cost, and how long it will take — whether you're choosing a diamond or bringing in a chain that broke twenty years ago.

  • Fine Italian gold and silver jewellery
  • GIA-certified diamonds
